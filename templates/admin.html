One-time Dropbox Setup
This works on restricted networks — the Pi only makes outbound calls and never needs to be reachable from the internet.
1
Create a Dropbox app at dropbox.com/developers/apps → Scoped access → Full Dropbox.
Enable: files.metadata.read and files.content.read.
Under Redirect URIs add: http://localhost/dropbox-auth
2
Enter your App Key and Secret and save:
3
Open the authorization link in any browser on any device. Log in to Dropbox and click Allow.
